myHoliday

I worked at the boys' dormitory during the holidays because I was lazy. It was better for me to work in the dormitory to earn money to reduce the burden on parents, so I decided to work during the holidays. During the holidays, my fellow workers were very compact and good. We started working from morning until evening, and every day, our fellow workers were very compact. So, after we finished work, we exercised together. My workmates played football behind the dormitory.

We rarely had breakfast during our holiday because we were very lazy to wake up early to cook. By 7 o'clock, we already started working, and we only drank milk every morning. After finishing work, we cooked with friends, all helping each other — some cooked rice, some washed dishes, and others cooked the side dishes.

During the holidays, we did everything together, so every job was very enjoyable. After working for almost 4 months before the start of lectures, we went to Waterworld with fellow workers to relax. We were tired of working in the dormitory and wanted to enjoy ourselves before classes started. We spent the day from morning until evening, and we were very happy. This is my holiday story — everyone helped each other and stayed close. That's my experience during the holidays.
